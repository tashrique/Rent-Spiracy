SAMPLE RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

THIS LEASE AGREEMENT (the "Agreement") made this 1st day of May, 2023, by and between:

LANDLORD: ABC Properties LLC ("Landlord")
TENANT: John Doe ("Tenant")

WITNESSETH:

PROPERTY: Landlord hereby leases to Tenant, and Tenant hereby leases from Landlord, for residential purposes only, the premises located at 123 Main Street, Apt 4B, Anytown, USA 12345 (the "Premises").

TERM: The term of this Agreement shall be for a period of 12 months, commencing on May 1, 2023, and ending on April 30, 2024 ("Initial Term").

RENT: Tenant shall pay to Landlord as rent for the Premises the sum of $1,500.00 per month, due on the first day of each month, without demand, deduction, or offset. Rent must be paid in full and not in partial payments.

SECURITY DEPOSIT: Upon execution of this Agreement, Tenant shall deposit with Landlord the sum of $3,000.00 as a security deposit for the performance by Tenant of the terms of this Agreement. Landlord may, but shall not be obligated to, apply the security deposit toward the cost of remedying any default by Tenant under this Agreement.

LATE CHARGES: If Tenant fails to pay any installment of rent within 3 days after such payment is due, Tenant shall pay a late charge of $100.00 plus $20.00 per day for each day payment is late.

UTILITIES: Tenant shall be responsible for payment of all utilities and services to the Premises, including electricity, gas, water, sewer, trash, telephone, internet, and cable TV.

TENANT'S OBLIGATIONS:
(a) Tenant shall not make any alterations, additions, or improvements to the Premises without Landlord's prior written consent.
(b) Tenant shall keep the Premises in a clean and sanitary condition and shall properly dispose of all trash and garbage.
(c) Tenant shall not disturb, annoy, endanger, or inconvenience other tenants of the building or neighbors.
(d) Tenant shall not use the Premises for any illegal or immoral purposes.
(e) Tenant shall allow Landlord access to the Premises at any time without prior notice for inspection or repairs.
(f) Tenant shall not sublet or assign the Premises without Landlord's prior written consent.

LANDLORD'S RIGHT OF ENTRY: Landlord or Landlord's agents may enter the Premises at any time without securing Tenant's permission in case of emergency, and at reasonable times for the purpose of making repairs or improvements, or to show the Premises to prospective buyers, tenants, or lenders.

PETS: Absolutely NO PETS of any kind are allowed on the Premises, even temporarily, without Landlord's prior written consent.

PARKING: Parking is restricted to the following areas: designated parking space #42. Tenant is not permitted to wash, repair, or paint in this parking area or any other part of the building. Only vehicles that are operational and currently registered may park in this space.

MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS: Tenant acknowledges that the Premises are in good order and repair, unless otherwise indicated. Tenant shall, at Tenant's own expense, maintain the Premises in a clean and sanitary manner, including all equipment, appliances, furniture, and furnishings therein, and shall surrender the same, at the termination of this Agreement, in as good condition as received, normal wear and tear excepted.

ATTORNEY'S FEES: If any legal action or proceeding is brought by either party to enforce any part of this Agreement, the prevailing party shall recover, in addition to all other relief, reasonable attorney's fees not to exceed $2,000.00.

ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS:
1. Tenant must pay a non-refundable application fee of $500.00 prior to move-in.
2. Tenant must provide proof of wire transfer for first and last month's rent, plus security deposit, within 24 hours of signing this agreement.
3. Landlord is not responsible for any personal items or belongings of Tenant left on the Premises.
4. Tenant acknowledges that the property may contain lead-based paint and agrees to hold Landlord harmless for any damages.
5. Tenant agrees to pay for all locksmith charges if keys are lost or Tenant is locked out.
6. Tenant must notify Landlord via certified mail at least 60 days before the end of the lease term if Tenant intends to vacate the Premises.

ABANDONMENT: If Tenant abandons or vacates the Premises, Landlord may at his option terminate this Agreement, enter the Premises, remove and store any property left by Tenant, and re-rent the Premises. Abandonment is defined as absence of the Tenant from the Premises for 7 consecutive days while rent is due and Tenant's possessions remain in the Premises.

ENTIRE AGREEMENT: This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties, and no promises or representations, other than those contained herein, have been made. Any modifications to this Agreement must be in writing and signed by both Landlord and Tenant.

THE UNDERSIGNED TENANT HEREBY ACKNOWLEDGES RECEIPT OF A COPY OF THIS AGREEMENT.
